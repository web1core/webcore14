Welcome to TechShala

Tech Shala is an initiative by Vidyalankar, an educational institution based in Mumbai, India, that provides training and education in the field of technology. Tech Shala offers various courses and training programs in areas such as data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and full-stack development. The courses are designed to provide hands-on experience to students, and they include live projects, case studies, and industry interactions. The faculty at Tech Shala includes industry experts and experienced trainers, who provide guidance and mentorship to the students. Some of the courses offered by Tech Shala include: Certificate Course in Data Science Certificate Course in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Certificate Course in Full-Stack Web Development Certificate Course in Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking Certificate Course in Cloud Computing The duration of these courses varies from 3 months to 9 months, and they are open to students and working professionals. Tech Shala also offers placement assistance to its students, and many of them have secured jobs in reputed companies after completing their courses. Overall, Tech Shala is a reputed institution for technology education and training in Mumbai, and it is a popular choice among students and professionals who want to upskill in the field of technology.

TechShala is a platform for college students to showcase their technical skills and participate in various technical events happening on their campus
