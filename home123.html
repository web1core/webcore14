WELCOME TO TECHSHALA

VIDYALANKAR POLYTECHNIC

TechShala is a technical community for college students, providing opportunities for students to showcase their technical skills and learn from each other. We organize various technical events, workshops, and seminars on different topics such as coding, web development, app development, and more. Tech Shala is an initiative by Vidyalankar, an educational institution based in Mumbai, India, that provides training and education in the field of technology. Tech Shala offers various courses and training programs in areas such as data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and full-stack development. The duration of these courses varies from 3 months to 9 months, and they are open to students and working professionals. Tech Shala also offers placement assistance to its students, and many of them have secured jobs in reputed companies after completing their courses. Overall, Tech Shala is a reputed institution for technology education and training in Mumbai, and it is a popular choice among students and professionals who want to upskill in the field of technology.
